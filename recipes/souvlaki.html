Chicken Souvlaki

Home

You ready for this?

This dinner is badass. Pair with your favorite badass beer and fries. May i recommend dipping your fries in the tzatziki sauce!

Ingredients

  • Kabobs

    • ¼ cup olive oil
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • ½ teaspoon salt
    • 1 ½ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 6 wooden skewers, or as needed

  • Sauce

    • 1 (6 ounce) container plain Greek-style yogurt
    • ½ cucumber - peeled, seeded, and grated
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 2 teaspoons white vinegar
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • 1 pinch salt

Directions

  • Step I

    • Gather all ingredients.

  • Step II

    • Prepare kabobs:
      1. Combine olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, oregano, and salt in a large resealable bag.
      2. Add chicken, coat with the marinade, squeeze out excess air and seal the bag; marinate in the refrigerator for 2 hours.

  • Step III

    • Prepare sauce:
      1. Mix yogurt, cucumber, olive oil, vinegar, garlic, and salt together in a bowl.
      2. Refrigerate sauce for flavors to blend, 1 to 2 hours.

  • Step IV

    • Soak wooden skewers in a bowl of cold water for about 15 minutes. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.

  • Step V

    • Remove chicken from marinade and thread onto the soaked skewers. Discard unused marinade.

  • Step VI

    • Cook the skewers on the preheated grill, turning frequently until browned on all sides and chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 7 to 8 minutes per side.

  • Step VII

    • Serve with tzatziki sauce. Enjoy!
